About Me

My name is Brian Lipsett. I am from Newmarket, NH and am currently pursuing a career in web development. After high school I attended Lakes Region Community College in Laconia, NH. During my first year of the Business Management program I began work at Blake's Restaurant in Manchester.

3 years into my career in hospitality I decided I needed a change and enlisted into the New Hampshire Air National Guard. Over the past 6 years I have dedicated all my time and effort into the Guard and USAF. Visiting multiple countries and various U.S. locations the temporary duty assignments and deployments I learned of my passion for travel and making the most of only a few hours in a new foreign city.
